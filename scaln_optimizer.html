Physical model and references:
ScAlN biaxial modulus: M_bi(x) = C11+C12-2*C13²/C33, polynomial fit M_bi(x) = -1430x²-264x+471 [GPa] (x=Sc fraction, valid 0≤x≤0.375). Source: S. Zhang, W.Y. Fu, D. Holec, C.J. Humphreys, M.A. Moram, Journal of Applied Physics 114, 243516 (2013). DOI: 10.1063/1.4848036. CTE matching point: Sc ≈ 33.2% (alpha_ScAlN = alpha_Sapphire = 5.0 ppm/K, using alpha_ScN=6.61 ppm/K from Ciprian et al. 2024). Note: Room temperature DFT values. Cryogenic (4K) M_bi values not reported. Previous model (E/0.78) underestimated M_bi by 19-22% for Sc<30%.
LiNbO3 elastic constants: Weis, R.S. & Gaylord, T.K. Applied Physics A 37, 191 (1985).
AlN CTE: alpha_AlN=4.2 ppm/K — Davydov, V.Yu. et al. (2002). ScN CTE: alpha_ScN=6.61±0.60 ppm/K (300K, XRD) — Ciprian, Mihalic, Lüttich, Hörich, Wade, Christian, Dadgar, Ambacher, Appl. Phys. Lett. 124, 052203 (2024). DOI: 10.1063/5.0176082. CTE matching point: Sc≈33.2% based on linear interpolation using alpha_ScN=6.61 ppm/K (Ciprian et al. APL 2024). Important caveat: Lu et al. APL Mater. 6, 076105 (2018) measured ScAlN CTE experimentally as 4.18–4.65 ppm/K (all Sc concentrations), which is below sapphire CTE (5.0 ppm/K) for all compositions. If Lu et al. 2018 is correct, a CTE-matched ScAlN/sapphire configuration may not exist. The linear interpolation of pure ScN CTE (Ciprian 2024) to ScAlN alloy may not account for bowing effects. CTE matching point prediction has high uncertainty.
Bond stress limit 23 MPa: K. Watanabe & R. Takigawa, Applied Surface Science 620, 156666 (2023). DOI: 10.1016/j.apsusc.2023.156666
